## Top Stories

**SpaceX files publicly for Nasdaq IPO under ticker SPCX.** Elon Musk's rocket and satellite company took the first formal step toward what could be the largest IPO in history, with a potential valuation north of $1.75 trillion and a listing as early as June 12. The offering could raise up to $75 billion. ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/15/spacex-ipo-public-filing-elon-musk-trading-debut-ticker-spcx/), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-20/musk-s-spacex-files-publicly-for-nasdaq-ipo-under-symbol-spcx))

**OpenAI preparing confidential IPO filing as soon as Friday.** The ChatGPT maker, valued at $850B+ by private investors, is working with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley on a draft prospectus. The company is targeting a September debut, just days after defeating Elon Musk's lawsuit challenging its corporate structure. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/openai-ipo-filing.html), [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/20/openai-ipo-spacex-musk))

**Nvidia beats Q1 estimates but gives lackluster forward guidance.** Revenue jumped 85% year-over-year to $81.6B, beating the $78.8B consensus, with EPS of $1.87 vs. $1.76 expected. However, the sales forecast disappointed investors amid mounting chip competition. The company added $80B to its buyback plan. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/nvidia-nvda-earnings-report-q1-2027.html))

**Meta lays off ~7,900 employees — 10% of its workforce.** Zuckerberg wrote in a memo that "success isn't a given" as the company accelerates its AI-first pivot, with capex projected to reach $145B in 2026. About 7,000 employees will be moved into AI-focused roles. More cuts expected in August. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/20/meta-layoffs-zuckerberg-says-success-isnt-a-given-in-memo.html))

**Intuit slashes 17% of workforce — over 3,000 jobs — to refocus on AI.** The TurboTax and QuickBooks maker is restructuring around AI partnerships with OpenAI and Anthropic. Shares fell 11% after hours despite strong Q2 revenue of $4.65B (up 17% YoY). ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/20/intuit-to-lay-off-over-3000-employees-to-refocus-on-ai/))
